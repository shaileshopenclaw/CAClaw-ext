# Audit Starter Checklist

## Objective

Use this checklist to organize audit work before detailed review.

## Checklist

- Confirm the engagement objective and current stage.
- List requested records and pending support.
- Capture workpaper areas that need attention.
- Record review questions and exceptions.
- Prepare next actions for the team or client.

## Output

- audit prep checklist
- evidence request list
- review and exception tracker
