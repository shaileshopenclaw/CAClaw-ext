# Audit Starter Guide

## What This Workflow Covers

Use this workflow for audit planning, evidence request preparation, workpaper support, and review structure.

## Starter Sequence

1. Clarify whether the task is planning, evidence collection, workpaper prep, or review.
2. Identify what support, documentation, or follow-up is needed.
3. Use the checklist asset to organize the audit work.
4. Produce a review-oriented summary for the CA or engagement team.

## Keep In Scope

- planning checklists
- evidence request lists
- workpaper preparation structure
- review and follow-up prompts

## Keep Out Of Scope

- automated audit claims
- final assurance conclusions
- anything that implies the checklist replaces audit judgment
