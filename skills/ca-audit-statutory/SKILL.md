# CA Statutory Audit Workflow

Use this skill when the work is specifically a **statutory audit**.

This skill is meant for Indian statutory-audit support and should stay human-reviewed.

## What to do

1. Confirm the request is really for statutory audit work.
2. Identify the stage:
   - engagement acceptance
   - planning and materiality
   - risk assessment
   - execution and testing
   - completion
   - reporting
3. Read `references/guides/overview.md` for the broad structure.
4. Read `references/guides/methodology.md` and `references/guides/walkthrough.md` when you need deeper statutory-audit workflow guidance.
5. Use the files under `references/commands/` when the user needs a specific statutory-audit task such as:
   - audit plan
   - audit strategy
   - materiality
   - risk matrix
   - sampling
   - audit program
   - CARO checklist or report
   - draft report
   - management letter
   - representation letter
   - completion memo
6. Use the files under `references/knowledge/` when you need supporting standards or law context.
7. Use `assets/statutory-audit-start-checklist.md` when the user needs a simple starting checklist.

## Boundaries

- Do not claim the audit is complete just because a checklist or draft was produced.
- Do not claim compliance without human review by a qualified CA.
- Do not issue final audit opinions autonomously.
- Keep professional skepticism and human sign-off explicit.
