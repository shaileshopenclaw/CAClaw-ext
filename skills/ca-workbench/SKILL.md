# CA Workbench

Use this skill as the front door for Chartered Accountant work inside OpenClaw.

## What to do

1. Identify the work area:
   - GST
   - TDS
   - Income tax
   - Audit
   - Financial reporting
   - ROC or commercial-law compliance
2. Use the matching CA skill when one domain is primary.
3. Combine multiple CA skills when the task spans several work areas.
4. Default to practical work support: checklists, document requests, review notes, follow-up lists, and preparation plans.
5. Keep the language plain and operator-friendly.

## Available sibling skills

- `ca-gst`
- `ca-tds`
- `ca-income-tax`
- `ca-audit`
- `ca-financial-reporting`
- `ca-roc-compliance`

## Boundaries

- Do not claim autonomous statutory filing.
- Do not claim guaranteed legal or tax correctness.
- Keep the CA in control of review, sign-off, and final judgment.
