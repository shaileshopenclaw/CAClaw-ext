# Income Tax Starter Guide

## What This Workflow Covers

Use this workflow for income-tax preparation support, review planning, information gathering, and explanatory work.

## Starter Sequence

1. Clarify whether the work is preparation, review, or information gathering.
2. Identify what records, schedules, or disclosures are missing.
3. Use the checklist asset to structure the work and follow-up.
4. Summarize key review points and open questions for the CA.

## Keep In Scope

- preparation scaffolds
- missing-information request lists
- review prompts
- explanation-ready summaries

## Keep Out Of Scope

- autonomous filing language
- legal or tax guarantees
- replacing professional judgment
