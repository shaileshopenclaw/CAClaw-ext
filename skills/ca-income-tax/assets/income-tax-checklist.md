# Income Tax Starter Checklist

## Objective

Use this checklist to prepare income-tax work before final review or filing.

## Checklist

- Confirm the entity or individual scope and the filing objective.
- Gather records, schedules, and working notes.
- Capture open questions and missing support.
- List review points that need CA sign-off.
- Prepare the follow-up note for remaining information.

## Output

- prep checklist
- missing-information tracker
- review summary
