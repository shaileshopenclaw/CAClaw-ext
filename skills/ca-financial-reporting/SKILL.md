# CA Financial Reporting Starter Workflow

Use this skill for financial reporting and close support.

## What to do

1. Clarify whether the user is preparing, reviewing, reconciling, or packaging reporting work.
2. Read `references/financial-reporting.md` for workflow scope and safe-use boundaries.
3. Use `assets/financial-reporting-checklist.md` for reusable close and reporting scaffolds.
4. Keep the output practical, reusable, and plain-language.

## Boundaries

- No automated-signoff claims.
- No guarantee of reporting correctness.
- Support preparation and review, not final professional responsibility transfer.
