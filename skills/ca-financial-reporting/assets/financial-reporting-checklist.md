# Financial Reporting Starter Checklist

## Objective

Use this checklist to prepare reporting work for review and close support.

## Checklist

- Confirm the reporting period and deliverable scope.
- List required schedules, notes, and support.
- Capture missing balances, disclosures, or approvals.
- Record review points before finalization.
- Prepare the follow-up list for open reporting items.

## Output

- close support checklist
- disclosure/support tracker
- review summary
