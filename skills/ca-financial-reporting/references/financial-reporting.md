# Financial Reporting Starter Guide

## What This Workflow Covers

Use this workflow for close support, statement review, schedule preparation, note drafting, and reporting follow-up.

## Starter Sequence

1. Clarify whether the task is close preparation, statement review, or reporting support.
2. Identify missing schedules, support, or disclosure inputs.
3. Use the checklist asset to structure the work.
4. Summarize review points and pending actions for the CA.

## Keep In Scope

- close support
- schedule and note scaffolds
- statement review prompts
- reporting follow-up

## Keep Out Of Scope

- automated sign-off language
- guarantees of reporting correctness
- replacing review judgment
