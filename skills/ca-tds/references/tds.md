# TDS Starter Guide

## What This Workflow Covers

Use this workflow for TDS preparation, review, deduction checks, due-date readiness, and follow-up on missing support.

## Starter Sequence

1. Confirm whether the task is preparation, deduction review, or follow-up.
2. Identify supporting information or challan/data gaps.
3. Use the checklist asset to structure the work.
4. Prepare a review summary and next-action list for the CA.

## Keep In Scope

- deduction review
- due-date readiness
- supporting-data checks
- follow-up planning

## Keep Out Of Scope

- automated filing
- guarantees of statutory correctness
- advice presented as a final legal determination
