# TDS Starter Checklist

## Objective

Use this checklist to prepare TDS work for review and follow-up.

## Checklist

- Confirm the period, deductor details, and work objective.
- Gather deduction, challan, and return-prep inputs.
- Record missing entries, mismatches, or support gaps.
- List review questions before filing or submission.
- Prepare a follow-up list for clients or internal teams.

## Output

- TDS review checklist
- support-gap list
- follow-up action list
