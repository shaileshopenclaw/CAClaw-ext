# GST Starter Checklist

## Objective

Use this checklist to prepare GST work before final review or filing.

## Checklist

- Confirm the filing period and entity scope.
- Gather sales, purchase, and reconciliation inputs.
- Note missing data or unresolved mismatches.
- List review points before submission.
- Capture follow-up items for the client or internal team.

## Output

- filing-ready task list
- missing-information request list
- reconciliation follow-up notes
