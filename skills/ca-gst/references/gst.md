# GST Starter Guide

## What This Workflow Covers

Use this workflow when the user needs help preparing GST work, reviewing readiness, reconciling issues, or following up on missing information.

## Starter Sequence

1. Clarify the GST task type: preparation, reconciliation, review, or follow-up.
2. Identify what information or documents are missing.
3. Use the reusable checklist asset to organize the work.
4. Summarize open questions, review risks, and next actions for the CA.

## Keep In Scope

- readiness review
- reconciliation support
- information requests
- recurring compliance preparation

## Keep Out Of Scope

- autonomous filing claims
- legal guarantees
- pretending OpenClaw replaces statutory review
