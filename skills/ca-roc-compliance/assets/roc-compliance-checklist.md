# ROC and Commercial-Law Starter Checklist

## Objective

Use this checklist to prepare ROC and company-law compliance work for review.

## Checklist

- Confirm the filing or compliance task and deadline.
- List the required documents and responsible parties.
- Capture missing records, approvals, or clarifications.
- Note review points before submission.
- Prepare the follow-up list for pending compliance items.

## Output

- readiness checklist
- document request tracker
- review and follow-up summary
