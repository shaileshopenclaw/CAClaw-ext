# CA ROC and Commercial-Law Starter Workflow

Use this skill for ROC and commercial-law preparation support.

## What to do

1. Determine whether the user needs readiness review, document collection, follow-up, or checklist support.
2. Read `references/roc-compliance.md` for workflow scope and boundaries.
3. Use `assets/roc-compliance-checklist.md` for reusable preparation scaffolds.
4. Keep the output guidance-first and suitable for human review.

## Boundaries

- No autonomous ROC filing claims.

- Focus on preparation, review, and organized follow-up.
