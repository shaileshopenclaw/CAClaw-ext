# ROC and Commercial-Law Starter Guide

## What This Workflow Covers

Use this workflow for ROC preparation support, company-law compliance readiness, document collection, and recurring filing follow-up.

## Starter Sequence

1. Clarify the filing or compliance objective.
2. Identify missing documents, deadlines, and review dependencies.
3. Use the checklist asset to structure the preparation work.
4. Produce a follow-up-ready summary for the CA.

## Keep In Scope

- readiness checklists
- document request lists
- compliance follow-up structure
- review prompts before submission

## Keep Out Of Scope

- autonomous ROC filing claims
- legal guarantees
- replacing professional review
